French Toast

Home

Description:

Favorite breakfast, egg soaked bread drowned in syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon sugar
  • 6 slices brioche bread (white is okay!)

Directions:

  1. Beat eggs in shallow bowl. Add milk, vanilla, and cinammon sugar, and beat until frothy.
  2. Soak each slice of bread in the egg mix, first on one side, then the other.
  3. Cook both sides in a greased non-stick skillet until golden.
