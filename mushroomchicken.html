Mushroom Chicken

Home

Description:

Tender Chicken breast smothered in a creamy mushroom sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Mushrooms
  • Fresh Garlic Cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Herbs
  • Heavy Cream
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Pat the chicken cutlets dry with paper towels. Drizzle with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Cook in a large skillet or pan set over medium-high heat until golden brown on both sides then remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook the mushrooms in olive oil and butter until golden brown and starting to caramelize.
  4. Add the minced garlic and herbs then stir in cream. Season with fresh lemon, salt and pepper and allow to simmer for 5 mins.
  5. Add the chicken back into the pan, cook for 5 minutes until cooked through and tender.
